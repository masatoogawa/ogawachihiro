I have received my badge as a "Tokutei Gyoseishoshi" (Certified Administrative Procedures Legal Specialist). The badge is slightly larger than the standard one, and holding it reminds me once again of the gravity of my responsibilities.

![Tokutei Gyoseishoshi Badge](/blog/03292026/title.jpg)

With the legal amendments that came into effect in January 2026, the role of a Tokutei Gyoseishoshi has become even more vital. Previously, our authority to act as an agent in administrative appeals was limited to documents we had personally prepared. Now, this scope has been expanded: for any document that an Administrative Procedures Legal Specialist is authorized to create, we can provide broad support—even in cases where an individual's own application was rejected.

As a Tokutei Gyoseishoshi, I will remain focused and continue to dedicate myself to my studies to better protect the rights of my clients.
